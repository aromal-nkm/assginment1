Między występkiem a mitycznością

by Mary bolgh

Między występkiem a mitycznością by Mary Bolch is a thought-provoking examination of the intersection between crime and myth in contemporary society. The book explores how criminal behavior and societal responses to it are often influenced by cultural myths and narratives. Bolch investigates the ways in which myths, legends, and societal expectations shape our understanding of crime and deviance. Through a blend of theoretical analysis and case studies, Bolch highlights the complex relationship between criminal acts and the mythological frameworks that societies use to interpret them. The book delves into how myths can both perpetuate and challenge perceptions of criminality, and how these narratives impact legal and social responses to crime. Overall, Między występkiem a mitycznością provides a nuanced perspective on the ways in which cultural myths and societal beliefs intersect with the concept of crime, offering insights into the broader implications of this relationship for justice and societal norms.
